Discord

My biggest projects stem from pre-existing platforms with Discord leading the charge. I host my own "server" on discord where all of my friends and hundreds of other people can hang out in text or voice chats. Using this platform I have been able to develop engaging bots that you can use to play games, listen to music, and interact this others, using JavaScript and Discord as the UI.

Launch Box

Launch Box is a tool that allows you to host all of your PC video games into one central front-end software. I have used this app to develop my own branch of the software to manage my video game collection how I find most useful. The majority of this project does not involve any code, instead it's more of a creative project. I currently have almost 20,000 games across over 30 different platforms organized into my Launch Box.

Notion

Notion is the heart and soul of my projects. Notion is an online platform advertised as a "note taking app." However, it's essentially a website builder focused around productivity. I have been developing my own website for a few years as it has become like a second brain for me. Some features include a habit tracker, finance tracker, project matrix, note taking library, and so much more.

Personal Projects
